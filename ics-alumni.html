Having established our school roots in August 1994, Indian Central School has a legacy of nearly 30 years. Our former Icians have gone on to study at prestigious educational institutions in India and across the world then built their careers in various industries. Our school continues to develop an interest in our students, their dreams and their achievements. We believe it is essential that our students feel a sense of belonging and pride. One of the ways we are able to stay connected with our students is through our alumni association.

Our alumni association welcomes all former students to come together to celebrate memories shared at school and recount experiences after leaving ICS. We plan to host our reunion in the near future to commemorate the fabric that holds us together.

 

Vision of Alumni Association


Inspire former students to be ambassadors who foster a spirit of loyalty for the Indian Central School and engage with the community to support current students in their pursuit toward excellence.



Purpose

1. To inspire current students through the career and life paths of former students.

2. To share fond memories of ICS by maintaining relationships with former students and creating opportunities to reunite.


Former Teachers


We welcome former teachers of ICS to also be a part of our alumni so that they can maintain their friendships with each other. We recognise teachers that have made valuable service to Indian Central School and would like to give them an opportunity to reconnect with us particularly on special occasions.


 
About

Indian Central School is a progressive primary and secondary school affiliated to CBSE, Delhi. The aim of the school is to provide a holistic education in training the mind, body and soul of every child.
