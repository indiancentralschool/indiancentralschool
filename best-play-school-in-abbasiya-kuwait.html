ICS Play School

  1. Home
  2. ICS Play School

As one of the top kids play schools in Kuwait, ICS Play School firmly believes in engaging children through fun activities that can keep them active mentally and physically. We help children grow their intellect through experiential learning, which also plays a vital role in supporting children to better understanding the basics. With a well-rounded curriculum comprising play and social interaction activities, we show commitment to let children discover childhood in all spheres and develop a lifelong love for learning.

ICS Play School is well-equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and provides a wide range of activities for our little learners to have fun while learning and explore ways to overcome challenges. Activities are classified into two categories to encourage the development of motor skills and creative skills in kids. Below is the list of activities offered at ICS Play School

 

Activities for Motor Development


Building Blocks

Paper Tracing & Pasting

Sports & Play-Based Learning

Muscle Development Activities

Indoor & Outdoor Play Area


Activities for Creative Development


Music & Movement

Art & Craft

Drawing, Colouring & Scribbling

Clay Modelling

Sand Play/ Sand Paper Tracing



By engaging in these activities, children will develop a sense of belonging, friendship and brotherhood among them, and get the opportunity to experience healthy competition and gain a hunger for winning and feel pride in accomplishments. ICS Play School's playschool program is open for all the kids aged within the limit of 2 1/2 - 3 1/2, and it will be held inside the campus of Indian Central School, Kuwait. Hurry up! Admissions open now, and only limited seats are available. Enrol today and give your child a bright start


 

About

Indian Central School is a progressive primary and secondary school affiliated to CBSE, Delhi. The aim of the school is to provide a holistic education in training the mind, body and soul of every child.
